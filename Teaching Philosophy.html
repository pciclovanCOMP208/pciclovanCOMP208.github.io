Teaching Philosophy

Philip Ciclovan

I believe that every child has the right to a safe, supportive learning environment.

Further Details
Schools should be places where students are encouraged to discover and pursue their passions, forming them into future members of a productive society in which they are able to contribute to the betterment of the world through the sharing of their developed skills and knowledge.

Students should be engaged through the use of collaborative tasks,
project-based learning, and problem-solving with roots in real-world issues.
Design tasks should be multi-disciplinary, encouraging the seeking of multiple perspectives to refine ideas, and acknowledging the benefit of diversity within the classroom. Students should strive to be respectful of their peers and teachers;
mirroring the attitudes that they should practice in their everyday lives.

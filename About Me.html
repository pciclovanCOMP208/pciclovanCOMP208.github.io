About Me

Third Year Education Student
This is the third year that I am studying at the Strathfield Campus of the Australian Catholic University.

Summary

I have always been fascinated by both the Arts and Sciences, and consistently demonstrated enthusiasm for thoroughly understanding any topic which draws my interest. For this reason, I have pursued degrees in both the Arts (Creative Arts)
and Science (Biology). Within these degrees, I also studied many of the '-ologies': Anthropology, Sociology, Psychology.
My current double-degree program will enable me to contribute to society through empowering young people to become future leaders in the world of Design and Technology - particularly through my specialisation as a Food Technology and Textiles teacher
in NSW secondary schools. Through my Computing minor, I will also be able to teach the new Computing Technologies Syllabus.

I have a long-standing affinity for Music, Theatre, Performance, Literature, Visual Arts, and the Creative Process.
I am guided by logic in all that I do, favouring clear processes and well-developed plans. Despite enjoying creating opportunities for other people, and understanding the importance of accommodating the needs of target clients, I am an introvert at heart:
able to happily work alone, or as part of a small, cohesive team.
